Retail Reimagined
Triple Five Worldwide's American Dream aims to redefine the entertainment and retail experience.

By Evan Clark on October 19, 2018
An exterior rendering of American Dream

"Unrivaled." That is how the team at Triple Five Worldwide, owners of Mall of America and West Edmonton Mall, describe its latest entertainment & retail destination, American Dream. By taking best practices learned across its portfolio and fusing them with a fresh perspective, American Dream, located less than five miles from Manhattan, at the Meadowlands in New Jersey, combines world-class retail, entertainment, destination dining, and the latest in technology into a one-of-a-kind, must see, new customer experience.

American Dream, set to open in 2019, will draw on a diverse and broad consumer demographic located in a greater NY-NJ metro market area that generates more than $136 billion in sales annually. The property's close proximity and direct connections to New York City, comprised of over 20 million residents and hosting over 65 million annual visitors, is one of the key attributes that make this property able to claim 40 million annual visitors with a tourist draw projected at 50%. A major point of distinction for American Dream lies in the tenant mix with 55 percent entertainment attractions and 45 percent retail.

In terms of the retail offerings, fashion flagships will be center stage, along with high-end branded shops.
